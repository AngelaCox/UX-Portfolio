About Me

Originally from New Zealand, I've travelled and worked as a technical writer for over five years. I've worked across many industries including software, music performance, rail, psychology, insurance and data centres. I love writing because I can be a terrible introvert, though I do actually enjoy talking to people. Hobbies include ridiculous things like researching new doc piplines and reading shameful amounts of books. I also like normal things like writing things that nobody will read, playing my piano, riding my bike and swimming.
